This document is intended as a chapter in the first part of ``An Analytic History of Philosophical Amalysis'' \cite{rbjb001}.
It is made available as a separate document prior to publication of the whole and serves also as a guide to the other parts of the work in progress.

At this time the work as a whole is intended ultimately to fall into three parts, provisionally entitled:

\begin{enumerate}
\item To the Tractatus
\item The Twentieth Century
\item Theory Listings
