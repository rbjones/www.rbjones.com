Substantial Interests:

(s211)
The register of substantial interests in shares of public companies.

Since this company is private there is no legal requirement to maintain a register of substantial interests. Since it is a single-member company it wouldn't be very interesting anyway.

Debenture Holders:

(s190)
The register of debenture holders.

There is in law no requirement to keep a register of debenture holders, though there are some constraints on how this is done if it is done.

As it happens, there are no debenture holders for this company.

Service Agreements:

(s318)
The register of directors' service agreements.

There are no service agreements to be filed under the act, since the sole director's contract can be terminated by the company within 12 months without payment of compensation (see s318(11)).
