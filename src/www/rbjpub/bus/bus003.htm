The Business Park

The Factasia business park is a vision of the future of Business, a resource for companies who want the future now, and links to those companies who best illustrate now the values, methods and technologies we expect to prevail in the future.

The Factasia business park gives special attention to the needs of Value Agents, Virtual Corporations, and to the exploitation of Virtual Brands and is home to The Factasia Corporation (as yet fictitious), the first Value Agent, Virtual Corporation and Virtual Brand.

First virtually resident corporation: Roger Bishop Jones Limited.
