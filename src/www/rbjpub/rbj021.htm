Factasy:
Factasia is a fantasy in earnest. To suggest a confusion between fact and fantasy I use the word Factasy. This page is a little brainstorm on where Factasia might go.
The Future: The overarching purpose of Factasia is to contribute to a design for the future of our world.
Home of Factasy, a new cyber-literary genre. Factasy hyper-muddles up fact and fantasy.
A Vision .. futuristic hype.
A Fantasy about Our Future
A dream or a nightmare. The struggle between virtue and evil, played out with new kinds of cyber-magic. Seeking to engage more than curiousity.
A Realistic Vision?
Meant in earnest. Our future is stranger than fantasy.
A Design for the Future
Factasia is a design for the future, working out what we want and how to make it all to work. You can see all the work in progress, you can join in. First thoughts on architecture
A Contribution to Creating the Future
Factasia is trying to make it happen. Factastic Future Engineering in action. This isn't just a fantasy, its the real thing.
A Living Hyper-Book, a Never Ending Story
Unlike paper books this one is a continuous creation; it just grows forever. Like "The Never Ending Story" it's a book which tries to pull you in, envelop you, engage you in creating the story, make you part of the story.
An Economic Force
