The Global SuperBrain as Hyper-Rational Artificial Intelligence. An intelligent automation of the entire field of a priori knowledge, including the whole of mathematics.
What is it?
The distributed implementation on global computer networks of the automation of deductive reasoning and its applications, encompassing logic, mathematics and its applications, particularly in engineering design.
How to build it
Start with a modern logical foundation system for mathematics, meld with a functional programming language and implement using a synthesis of compiler and proof technology.
How to exploit it
Formalise scientific and engineering modelling techniques and realise the automation of engineering design.
