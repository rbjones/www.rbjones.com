The Future of The Future

Roger Jones

A hypertext presentation of material prepared for a session at the ICL Engineering Conference, September 1997.

The topic is in fact the future of a variety of futurology which I have dubbed "future engineering", with particular interest in the role of creative individuals working together to shape the profound changes which we can expect in our world over the next few decades.
