Generalised Evolution

  • Generalised Darwinism applies to non-biological systems
    • but keeps all the same constraints, applying biological models to non-biological evolutionary processes
  • Generalised evolution weakens some of the constraints
    • Replication with engineered variation
    • Intelligent artificial selection processes
  • Intelligent architect speeds evolution
    • but might screw it up
  • Constraints need (re-)consideration
    • e.g. relevance of Evolutionary Stable Strategies, reachability constraints.
