Scenario Planning

  • Vehicle for strategic business consultancy
    • and some weaknesses flow from this
  • Limited depth multiple scenario
    • same scenarios used by many companies
  • Enables "future proofing" of strategy
    • arguably this is the purpose of scenario planning
  • Avoids taking a position
    • but variants, e.g. Glen Hiemstra's "preferred futures", differ
