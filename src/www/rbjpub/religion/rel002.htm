What is CyberFaith?
CyberFaith is a framework within which individuals can construct their own personal religion. It has a minimal core which emphasises the personal nature of faith, morality and purpose.

CyberFaith involves:

  1. faith in self
  2. faith in humanity
  3. the moral imperative on the individual to chose (preferably, to construct) his own value system (optionally as part of a religion)
  4. the moral imperative on the individual to seek his own fulfilment through the pursuit of worthwhile purposes
