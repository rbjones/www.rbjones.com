Introduction:
A list of works in progress. Later, links and notes will be added.
At present this is just a list of things I am working on. There are links to online tracks where they exist. The numbers in brackets are the year in which I hope to have a performance online (add 2000). Eventually I intend to add notes on individual pieces.
So long as there are only three test tracks up there (as at present) this whole thing has to be regarded as wild speculation. The whole balance is at present skewed away from Chopin, as I try to focus on easy things. As soon as I get comfortable I will probably shift back to Chopin.
