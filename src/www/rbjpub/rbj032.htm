Philosophical Logicism is a theme concerned with the role, scope and limits of logic. It encompasses subthemes such as Mathematical Logicism (the view that mathematics is logic), and Logicist Epistemology. The Global SuperBrain as AI considers logic as technology for intelligence in globally networked computer systems.
